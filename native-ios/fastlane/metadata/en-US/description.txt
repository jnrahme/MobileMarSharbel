Saint Charbel brings prayer, story, and daily stillness into one focused iPhone and iPad experience. Read a child-friendly illustrated storybook with narration, follow the Rosary with mystery-by-mystery guidance, and keep core prayers close at hand.

The app is built around the same Saint Charbel media library used on marsharbel.com, so story artwork, narration, and Rosary audio stay aligned with the website while feeling calmer and more intentional on mobile.

Use it for personal prayer, family prayer, catechism, or simple quiet time with Saint Charbel.
