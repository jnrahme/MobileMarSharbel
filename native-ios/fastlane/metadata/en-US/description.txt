Saint Charbel Mobile is a calm iPhone and iPad companion for prayer, story, and daily stillness.

The app brings together two focused experiences in one place: a child-friendly Saint Charbel storybook and a guided Rosary path for daily prayer. Read the story like a real book, follow the narration, open the mystery set for today, and keep core Rosary prayers close at hand.

Built as a native companion to marsharbel.com, the app keeps Saint Charbel content accessible in a simpler and more prayerful mobile experience.
