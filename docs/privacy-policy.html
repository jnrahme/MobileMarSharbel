Saint Charbel Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Saint Charbel is designed to provide story, prayer, and Rosary content with minimal data collection. The app streams media from marsharbel.com and does not require account creation.

What the app uses

  • Network access to load story artwork, narration audio, and Rosary audio from marsharbel.com.
  • Basic device-level app functionality required by iOS to play audio and render content.

What we do not collect in the app

  • No account registration data.
  • No payment information.
  • No precise location data.
  • No contact lists, photos, camera, or microphone input.

Third-party services

The app depends on marsharbel.com hosting for remote media delivery. If that hosting changes, the app content endpoints may change as well.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, use the support page linked in the App Store listing.
