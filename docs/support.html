Saint Charbel Support

Need help with the Saint Charbel app? This page is the support destination used in the App Store listing.

What to include in a support request

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The app version and build number.
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened instead.

Common issues

  • If audio or images do not load, confirm the device has internet access and try reopening the app.
  • If remote content is unavailable, marsharbel.com may be temporarily unreachable.
